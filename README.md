# Bright-Coffee-shop-sales-analysis
Data analysis project providing business insights for Bright Coffee Shop using SQL, Excel, and data visualization.

**Introduction**

This project analyzes historical transactional data from Bright Coffee Shop to provide actionable business insights for the newly appointed CEO. The primary goal is to identify revenue drivers, underperforming products and sales trends across time intervals, ultimately guiding strategic decisions to grow revenue and improve product performance.

**Topic**

Business Insights for Bright Coffee Shop using Historical Transactional Data The analysis leverages SQL, data visualization, and reporting tools to uncover patterns in product sales, customer demand, and operational efficiency.

**Objectives**

1.	Identify which products generate the most revenue.
2.	Determine peak time intervals for sales activity.
3.	Highlight sales trends across product categories and time buckets.
4.	Provide recommendations to improve sales performance and customer engagement.
5.	Suggest next steps for automation, scalability, and future growth.

**Tools used**

**Key takeaways**

•	Coffee and Tea are the top revenue drivers, especially in the morning hours.
•	Bakery performs well early in the day but declines later.
•	Evening sales are consistently weak across all categories.
•	Underperforming products (Flavours, Loose Tea, Packaged Chocolate) contribute little to overall revenue.
•	Weekends generally show stronger sales than weekdays.

**Recommendations**

1.	Optimize Peak Hours: Focus staffing, inventory, and promotions during mornings.
2.	Strengthen Core Products: Ensure consistent quality and expand premium coffee/tea offerings.
3.	Boost Underperforming Products: Bundle weaker items with best-sellers or run targeted promotions.
4.	Improve Evening Sales: Introduce evening-specific deals (happy hour, combo offers).
5.	Customer Engagement: Launch loyalty programs and collect feedback to refine product mix.
6.	Operational Efficiency: Automate daily sales dashboards and align staff schedules with demand.
7.	Growth Strategy: Explore expansion into commuter-heavy areas and leverage digital marketing.
